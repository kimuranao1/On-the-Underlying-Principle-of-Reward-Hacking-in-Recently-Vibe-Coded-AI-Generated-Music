AI Music Reward Hacking Theory

As someone who has studied music theory for many years, I can say that this is neither magic nor the privilege of AI.

It's simply a "realm of music theory that no one has ever observed before."

Hit songs always share structural commonalities. The problem is that humans haven't analyzed it.

See the invisible

For example, suppose there is a melody called "Do-Re-Mi." Most people only recognize it as "Do → Re → Mi."

However, the real analysis is the following element.

  • Pitch distance
  • Root reference position
  • Chord context
  • Rhythmic placement
  • Strong and weak beat position
  • Lyric meaning
  • Phonetic intensity

In other words, the essence of music is not the notes themselves, but the relationships between them.

Example Analysis: Moon River

1. Basic Information

  • Key: C Major
  • Time Signature: 3/4
  • Tempo: Approximately 84
  • Form: AABA
  • Range: Approximately 9th

2. Melodic Structure

Major Interval Design

Ascending Second → Ascending Third → Descending 2nd
  • Few leaps
  • Easy to sing
  • Mainly a step progression

Melodic gravity

  • Central note: G
  • Final return: C
  • Cadence: Fifth or 1st
  • Tonic return: High frequency

Psychological effect:A sense of security that there is always a place to return to

---

3. Rhythmic DNA

Dotted 2nd + 8th + 8th note
  • Although it's a waltz, it lacks a dance-like feel.
  • Beat boundaries are unclear.
  • Tends to have a late rhythm.

Auditory impression:Feelings of floating, the surface of water, and time standing still.

---

4. Harmonic structure

I → vi → IV → I

Characteristics:

  • Frequent use of secondary dominants.
  • II–V sequence.
  • Long duration of dominants.

Auditory impression is simple, but theoretically advanced.
